Dialogue Cards: My Weekend

Instructions: Print this page and cut along the dotted lines. Each pair gets one set of cards (Partner A + Partner B).
LEVEL GREEN (Basic) - Set 1

PARTNER A

1. Hello! What's your name?
2. I'm ___. How was your weekend?
3. What did you do on Saturday?
4. And on Sunday?
5. That sounds nice/cool/interesting!

PARTNER B

1. I'm ___. And you?
2. It was great/OK/boring!
3. On Saturday I ___ (VERB+ed).
I was at ___ (PLACE).
4. On Sunday I ___ (VERB+ed).
I was at ___ (PLACE).
5. Thank you! Bye!

WORD HELP:

VERBS:
play → played watch → watched visit → visited help → helped listen → listened
PLACES:
home the cinema the park the sports centre the shopping centre

LEVEL YELLOW (Standard)

PARTNER A

1. Greeting + Ask name
2. Say your name + Ask about weekend
3. Ask about Saturday activities
4. Ask about Sunday activities
5. Give positive feedback + Say goodbye

PARTNER B

1. Answer with name + Return question
2. Describe feeling + Give reason
3. Name 2 Saturday activities + places
4. Name 2 Sunday activities + places
5. Thank + Say goodbye

REMEMBER TO USE:

  • was/were for places: "I was at..." / "We were at..."
  • Past tense verbs with -ed: played, watched, visited, listened, helped
  • Time markers: On Saturday... / On Sunday... / In the morning/afternoon/evening...
