🗣️ Talking about Last Weekend 🗣️

Simple Past Dialogues - Activities in Town

📱 Example Dialogue

A: Hi! How was your weekend?
B: Great! I went to the cinema on Saturday.
A: Really? What did you watch?
B: I watched "Spider-Man". It was fantastic!
A: Cool! Who did you go with?
B: With my sister. What about you?
A: I played football at the sports centre.
B: Did you win?
A: Yes, we did! It was exciting! ⚽

💡 Dialogue Tips:

✅ Ask follow-up questions
✅ Use Simple Past forms
✅ Give details (when, who, how)
✅ Both partners should talk equally

📚 Simple Past Forms

to go → went
to play → played
to watch → watched
to visit → visited
to buy → bought
to listen → listened
